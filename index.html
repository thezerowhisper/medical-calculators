Why These Calculators Use Indian Guidelines

Many standard medical calculators use Western or WHO cutoffs which are not always appropriate for Indians. For example, Indians develop diabetes and cardiovascular disease at lower BMI values, so Indian guidelines define overweight as BMI ≥ 23 rather than ≥ 25. The rabies PEP schedule follows the 4-dose Essen regimen (Days 0, 3, 7, 28). Obstetric tools follow FOGSI and DIPSI protocols specific to Indian practice. These tools reflect Indian clinical guidelines throughout.

About These Tools

All tools are built and maintained by a practising doctor in India. Intended for clinical use by healthcare professionals and for patient education. No data is collected or stored on any server — all calculations happen in your browser. Vaccination records in the UIP tracker are saved only on your own device.
