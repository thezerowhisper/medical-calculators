UIP Vaccine Schedule India — Complete Guide

India's Universal Immunisation Programme (UIP) provides free vaccines from birth through 16 years. This tracker calculates all due dates and lets you mark each vaccine as given to track your child's immunisation progress.

How to Use This Tracker

Enter your child's date of birth and tap Generate Vaccine Schedule. All vaccine due dates are calculated automatically. Tap any vaccine row to mark it as given — you can also record the actual date it was administered. Your data is saved on your device.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the UIP vaccine schedule in India?

UIP provides free vaccines at birth, 6 weeks, 10 weeks, 14 weeks, 9 months, 12 months, 15–18 months, 2 years, 4–6 years, 10 years and 16 years — covering BCG, OPV, DPT, Hepatitis B, IPV, Hib, PCV, Rotavirus, MR, JE, Td and more.

Which vaccines are given at birth in India?

BCG, OPV-0 (zero dose), and Hepatitis B birth dose — all within 24 hours of birth ideally.

When is the MMR vaccine given in India?

MR (Measles-Rubella) is given at 9 months and 15–18 months under UIP. MMR (adding Mumps) is given at 12 months and 15–18 months under the IAP private schedule.

Are UIP vaccines free in India?

Yes — all UIP vaccines are free at government health centres, PHCs, CHCs, and Anganwadi centres across India.

Will my data be saved?

Yes — all marked vaccines and dates are saved in your browser's local storage. They will still be there the next time you open this page on the same device and browser.
